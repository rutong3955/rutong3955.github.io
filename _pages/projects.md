## Field Research Projects

**Community-based Support for Early Learning and Responsive Care for Children Aged 0-6 Years in China**  
*June 2016–Present*

Evaluating the impacts of a community-based Early Childhood Development (ECD) project on children's cognitive and non-cognitive development outcomes in 40 counties in China.

- Designed evaluation plan and survey questionnaires
- Conducted data collection and analysis
- Wrote policy briefs and articles

**Evaluating the Impact of Special-designed Music Class on Rural Minority Children's Non-cognitive Abilities: A Quasi-Experiment Study in Southwestern China**  
*October 2023–October 2024*

Evaluating the impacts of a music class intervention on students' non-cognitive skills in rural Southwest China.

- Designed questionnaires and trained enumerators
- Conducted data analysis and article writing

**Improving the Information Literacy of Students from Primary and Senior Schools in Northwestern China**  
*May 2023–Present*

Exploring an effective method of developing students' information literacy and identifying a practical implementation toolkit in Northwest China.

- Drafted funding proposal
- Designed questionnaires
- Conducted data analysis and article writing

**Identifying the Impacts and Mechanisms of a Computer-Assisted Learning (CAL) Intervention on Students' Educational Outcomes: A Randomized Controlled Trial in Northwestern China**  
*July 2019–August 2025*

Evaluating the impacts of a CAL intervention on students' academic performance, non-cognitive skills, and long-term returns in 202 rural schools in Northwest China.

- Drafted funding proposal
- Designed questionnaires and trained enumerators
- Organized and supervised data collection
- Conducted data cleaning and article writing

**Building a Foundation for Human Capital Development in the Field of Education in Western China**  
*September 2016–December 2019*

Established an Experimental Economics in Education (EEE) discipline and research platform in the field of education based in Western China.

- Drafted funding proposal and project's narrative reports

**Evaluating the Impact of Health Information Intervention on Students' Health and Educational Outcomes in Rural Tibetan Areas of China**  
*September 2016–December 2016*

Examining the effects of a health information intervention on students' health and academic performance in 58 rural schools in Tibetan areas of China.

- Designed study and questionnaires
- Trained enumerators
- Organized and supervised data collection

**Using Standardized Patients Method to Measure Healthcare Quality in Rural China**  
*February 2015–December 2015*

Three waves of panel data collected from 279 physicians from 209 village clinics and 654 physicians from 209 township health centers in three provinces of China.

- Designed questionnaires
- Trained enumerators and standardized patients
- Organized and supervised data collection
- Conducted data cleaning

**Seeing is Learning: Evaluating the Impact of Vision Care on Students' Academic Performance: A Randomized Controlled Trial in Rural China**  
*May 2014–June 2015*

Assessing the educational impact of providing eyeglasses to near-sighted students in 300 rural schools in Northwest China.

- Designed questionnaires
- Trained enumerators
- Organized and supervised data collection

**Nourishing the Future: Targeting Infants and Their Caregivers to Reduce Undernutrition and Improve Infants' Development: A Randomized Controlled Trial in Rural Northwestern China**  
*August 2014–May 2015*

Examining the effects of early intervention on infants' health and development outcomes in 1,802 rural households in Northwest China.

- Developed intervention materials
- Designed questionnaires
- Trained enumerators
- Organized and supervised data collection

**Using Conditional Cash Transfer (CCT) to Improve Maternal and Child Health (MCH) Services in Rural China**  
*August 2014–December 2014*

Understanding the current use of MCH services and strengthening the use of MCH services through CCT intervention in 27 minority villages in Northwest China.

- Designed questionnaires
- Trained enumerators
- Organized and supervised data collection
- Conducted data cleaning
